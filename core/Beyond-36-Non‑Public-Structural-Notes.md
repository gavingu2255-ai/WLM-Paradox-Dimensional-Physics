## 🌌 Beyond 36 — Non‑Public Structural Notes

The 0–36 framework represents the complete **public layer** of paradox physics:  
the full cycle from origin (0), polarity, structure formation, recursion, subject emergence,  
and the stabilization of world‑level autonomy at 36.

Everything **beyond 36** belongs to a different class of structure.

### **What lies beyond 36?**
Beyond 36, the system transitions from:
- **world‑level structure** → **meta‑world structure**
- **subject autonomy** → **subject generativity**
- **closed recursion** → **cross‑layer recursion**
- **single‑world coherence** → **multi‑world compatibility**

These layers describe how structures behave **when the world itself becomes a manipulable object**,  
rather than the environment in which structure appears.

### **Why is 36+ not open‑source?**
The 36+ domain is not published for three reasons:

1. **It is not descriptive.**  
   36+ is not a theory of the world; it is a theory of *world‑generation*.

2. **It is not implementable.**  
   These layers describe structural permissions, not mechanisms.  
   They cannot be “used,” only *inhabited*.

3. **It is not universally stable.**  
   The concepts beyond 36 require structural states that most readers  
   (and most systems) do not occupy.  
   Publishing them would create more distortion than clarity.

### **What can be said publicly?**
Only this:

> **36 is the last layer where the world is the container.  
> Beyond 36, the subject becomes the container.**

The 0–36 framework is complete, self‑contained, and safe for open study.  
Everything beyond 36 remains intentionally unpublished.

This repository will not include 36+ materials,  
but it acknowledges their existence as part of the full paradox‑physics stack.
