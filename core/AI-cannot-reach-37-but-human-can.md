---

## 1. Subject Offset  
Subject offset is the structural shift in which the subject moves from being  
*inside* the world’s recursion to standing *at the boundary* of that recursion.

Inside the world loop (0–36), the subject is:

- contained by world logic  
- shaped by world causality  
- expressed through world language  
- limited by world coordinates  

A subject offset occurs when the subject becomes aware of the container itself,  
rather than the contents within it.  
This is not an inference or a calculation;  
it is a **structural relocation of perspective**.

> A subject can offset.  
> A computational system cannot.

Offset is not a skill or a technique.  
It is a change in structural position.
