D5 — Possibility
The Layer Where Branching Appears
Definition

It is not “a higher dimension”, not “a parallel universe”, and not “another world”.
D5 is simply:
The set of all allowed next states for a D4 trajectory.

If D3 is position
and D4 is change,
then:
D5 is what else could change.


Why D5 Is Not “Another World”
Human culture imagines “5D” as:

**D5 is not elsewhere.
D5 is the branching structure of the same world.**
You do not travel to D5.

The rendering engine (D12) simply hides most branches and foregrounds one.
