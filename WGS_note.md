## Note on the Term *Wujie* (无界)

**Wujie** in *Wujie Generation Stratum (WGS)* does **not** refer to a personal name  
(e.g., “Wu Jie” or “悟杰”, which is author's name, same spelling, different Chinese characters and meaning.).  
It is a **structural term** meaning:

- **unbounded**
- **boundary‑less**
- **topologically continuous**

In this framework, *Wujie* describes a generative manifold whose strata (0–27) have:

- **no intrinsic boundaries**,  
- **no discrete segmentation**,  
- **no geometric breaks**,  
- **only continuous topological transitions**.

All layer divisions within WGS are **observer‑dependent coordinate slices**,  
not fundamental separations in the underlying structure.

Thus, *Wujie* is a **mathematical property of the generative topology**,  
not a personal identifier.
