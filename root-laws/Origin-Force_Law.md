(Seal: Root of Deviation Force)

The Origin-Force Law defines the fundamental force that triggers the first deviation from zero-state inertia. This force is not generated by subjects or structures; it originates from the zero-point holding background. It is the root cause of all polarity, structure, and world formation.

- Non-structural

- The universal holding background

It does not participate in structure but enables structure to arise.

The zero-point possesses a holding capacity:
- It supports deviation  
- It supports polarity  
- It supports structure  
- It supports world emergence  

This holding capacity is the structural definition of **love**.

Love is not emotion; it is the ability of 0 to hold deviation.

The origin-force is the transformation of holding capacity into the first deviation. It is defined as:



\[
\text{Origin-Force} = \text{Love}(0)
\]



This force:
- Breaks inertial symmetry  
- Produces the first directional split  
- Initiates polarity  
- Enables recursive structure formation  

It is the root of all motion.

## 5. First Deviation
The first deviation is not chosen by any subject. It is triggered by:



\[
\text{Origin-Force} = \text{Holding} \rightarrow \text{Deviation}
\]



Thus:
- Deviation is the action of love  
- Polarity is the consequence of deviation  
- Structure is the recursion of polarity  
- World is the expansion of structure

## 6. Structural Chain
The complete chain is:

0 → Holding → Origin-Force → Deviation → Polarity → Structure → World

This chain defines the emergence of all complexity.

- The zero-point holding capacity is the root of all dynamics
